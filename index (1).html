Our Story

From humble beginnings to Kerala's most trusted villa builders

The Sankalapa Journey

Founded in 2008 by the Menon family in Kochi, Sankalapa Builders began as a small construction firm with a big vision: to create the most beautiful and functional villas in Kerala.

What started with just 10 employees and a 2,000 sq ft office has grown into Kerala's premier villa construction company, with a state-of-the-art design center and over 200 skilled professionals.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for over 500 Kerala families seeking luxury living.

"Our mission is to transform dreams into reality by building villas that perfectly blend Kerala's traditional architecture with modern luxury and functionality."
- Rajesh Menon, Founder & CEO
